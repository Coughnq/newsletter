A journey lit by AI: Reflections from the Founder


Hi there! My name is Abner Sanabria Cruz, and I’m the Founder & CEO of Intelliquinte L.L.C.


In my first newsletter, I introduced Intelliquinte and shared some of the values and ideas that drive it. This time, I’d like to take you behind the scenes and share a bit about how I approach business tasks and navigate this journey.


First off, I’ll be honest—I often feel like I have no idea what I’m doing. And you know what? I think that feeling might stick around for a while. But that’s okay, because it keeps me curious and open to learning.


Now, let’s talk about something a bit controversial: Artificial Intelligence. Love it or hate it, it’s a tool that has played a significant role in my journey. For me, AI has been a guide, shining light on a dimly lit path. At first, it was like walking with a tiny matchstick, but over time, that light has grown—first into a candle, then a flashlight, and maybe someday, something even brighter.


So, how do I use AI? It helps me refine my ideas, critique my writing, and identify potential flaws. It sparks creativity when I hit a wall and serves as a reliable and consistent partner in this adventure. Honestly, without AI, the idea of starting a business might have stayed locked in the back of my mind forever. For me, it’s been a game-changer. Of course, like anything else, AI has its drawbacks—but I’ve found its positives outweigh them.


No matter where you stand on AI, I respect your perspective. I understand that it’s not the same as something handcrafted by human effort. But I hope my passion and love for what I’m building will fill that gap and create something meaningful.


This business has already taught me a lot—especially that I wasn’t as prepared as I thought I was! But that’s the beauty of it. I jumped in without looking back, and now I’m asking myself: How can I better prepare for what’s ahead? My first step is to document everything I know about Intelliquinte: its mission, values, and identity. I want to define what Intelliquinte would be if it were a person—its voice, character, and essence.


At the end of the day, if there’s one thing I hope to gain from this journey, it’s learning how to live a life that’s both fruitful and joyful—even in the face of challenges.


If you’ve made it this far, thank you for being part of this journey with me. I hope to connect with you again in the next newsletter. I may not change the world, but if I can bring a smile to even one person’s face, I’ll consider that a success.
